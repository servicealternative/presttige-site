Cookie Preferences

Effective date: April 23rd, 2026

This Cookie Preferences notice explains how Mettalix LLC FZ uses cookies and similar technologies on presttige.net. Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help websites function, remember preferences, analyse traffic, and support the user experience.

1. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Strictly necessary cookies — essential for the operation, security, and basic functionality of the Website;
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand how users interact with the Website and to improve performance;
  • Functionality cookies — used to remember certain preferences or improve user experience;
  • Third-party cookies — cookies placed by trusted external service providers where relevant.

2. Purposes of Cookie Use

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • ensure the Website operates correctly;
  • maintain security and integrity;
  • understand usage patterns and improve performance;
  • support user preferences and functionality;
  • facilitate analytics and future service optimisation.

3. Consent

Where required by applicable law, non-essential cookies should only be used on the basis of user consent. If future advertising, remarketing, or advanced analytics tools are implemented, an appropriate consent mechanism or cookie banner may be introduced.

3A. Consent Banner and Preference Centre

The Website includes a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept, reject, or customise optional cookie categories before non-essential analytics tools are loaded. You can change your choices at any time by selecting “Cookie Preferences” in the footer.

Strictly necessary cookies support core site function and cannot be switched off through the banner. Analytics cookies, including Google Analytics, are used only if you opt in. Marketing cookies are reserved for future tools and remain off unless selected.

You may also delete existing cookies directly through your browser settings. Browser controls usually allow you to remove cookies for a specific website, block future cookies, or clear stored site data.

4. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage, block, or delete cookies through browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect functionality, performance, or user experience.

5. Third-Party Tools

The Website may in the future rely on trusted third-party tools for analytics, forms, infrastructure, or performance measurement. Any such tools may place or access cookies in accordance with their own applicable privacy practices and legal requirements.

6. Updates to This Notice

We may update this Cookie Preferences notice from time to time. Any revised version will be posted on this page together with an updated effective date.
